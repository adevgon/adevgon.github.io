Welcome to AB Enterprises

Welcome to AB Enterprises, a Leading Innovator in Injection Molding Solutions

At AB, we specialize in the design and manufacture of high-quality injection molded products,
catering to a diverse range of industries including home appliances and automotive systems.
Established in 2014, our headquarters in Delhi serves as the center of our operations,
where precision engineering and advanced manufacturing techniques converge to deliver superior products.


Our Expertise: Axial Fans and Custom Injection Molded Components

Our flagship products include state-of-the-art axial fans, renowned for their
efficiency and reliability. Engineered for optimal performance, our fans play
a crucial role in enhancing air quality and comfort in homes and commercial settings.
Beyond axial fans, we provide tailored solutions for the home appliance and automotive
sectors, producing components that are integral to everyday functionality and innovation
in these fields.


Commitment to Quality and Innovation

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. AB is committed to maintaining the
highest standards of production. Our facilities are equipped with the latest
injection molding machines and technology, operated by a team of skilled professionals.
We are proud to be pivoted to our core pillars, underscoring our dedication to quality
assurance and customer satisfaction.


Sustainable Practices and Future Goals

Sustainability is a key component of our operational philosophy.
We strive to minimize our environmental impact through waste reduction,
energy-efficient practices, and continual improvement of our processes.
Looking forward, our goal is to expand our reach in international markets,
continuing to innovate while providing our customers with eco-friendly
and effective solutions.


Partner with Us

At AB, we believe in building strong relationships with our clients,
offering customizable options and collaborative design efforts to meet
the specific needs of each project. We invite you to explore the
possibilities with us. Whether you need a component for the next generation
of home appliances or advanced axial fans, we are here to provide solutions
that drive your success.
